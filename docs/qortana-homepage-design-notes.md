# Qortana Homepage Design Notes

Current approved design direction for the company homepage:

- Keep the homepage spacious. Avoid over-containerization and dense stacked UI.
- Hero should stay left-aligned with a long first line, a long second line, and the CTA directly underneath.
- Section headlines should prefer one line and never exceed two lines unless explicitly approved.
- Preserve the buttery, warm palette from the intake flow rather than reverting to stark black-and-white minimalism.
- Keep the subtle grid and stripe treatments, but let them feel continuous and intentional rather than boxed in.
- Navigation should feel premium and lightly elevated, not merely functional.
- Header structure should stay minimal: `QORTANA — Infrastructure — Results — Company — Book a Strategy Call`.
- Do not use a holistic header bar container; the header should be composed from standalone pills only.
- Nav hover behavior should use the `reset` pattern: duplicated label stack with vertical upward roll and a background fill that rises from the bottom.
- Primary CTA behavior should use the `reset` magnetic flight-button pattern, with Qortana-specific warm cream / gold / espresso colors.
- Motion should feel smooth and expensive, with restrained, tactile easing rather than flashy animation.
